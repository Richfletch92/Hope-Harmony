Who we are

Hope Harmony is a non-profit organization that helps people in need. We provide support, advice and resources to help people with mental health issues. Our team is made up of professionals who are dedicated to helping others. We believe that everyone deserves to live a happy and healthy life, and we are here to help you achieve that.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide support and resources to people who are struggling with mental health issues. We want to help people find the help they need to live a happy and healthy life. We believe that everyone deserves to be happy and healthy, and we are here to help you achieve that.

Our Values

At Hope Harmony, we believe in the power of kindness and compassion. We are dedicated to helping others and making a positive impact in the world. We believe that everyone deserves to be treated with respect and dignity, and we are committed to helping people in need. Our values are at the core of everything we do, and we are proud to be a part of an organization that is making a difference in the world.

Our Team

Nora Hart – Executive Director

Nora Hart is the Executive Director at Hope Harmony, where she leads the organization with a deep commitment to mental health advocacy. With over 15 years of experience in the non-profit sector, Nora oversees operations, fundraising, and community engagement. She is dedicated to ensuring that Hope Harmony provides essential support and resources to individuals facing mental health challenges, fostering a compassionate, client-centered environment. Nora’s leadership is driven by her belief in reducing stigma and promoting well-being for all.

Miguel Cortez – Outreach Coordinator

Miguel Cortez is the Outreach Coordinator at Hope Harmony, where he plays a key role in connecting the organization with local communities and raising awareness about mental health services. Miguel brings a passion for community engagement and a deep understanding of cultural nuances to his work. He has a unique ability to connect with diverse groups, ensuring that mental health resources are accessible to everyone. Miguel is dedicated to building partnerships, organizing events, and reaching individuals who may need support, making him a vital part of Hope Harmony’s mission.

Sierra Smith – Community Engagement Specialist

Sierra Smith is the Community Engagement Specialist at Hope Harmony, where she is passionate about raising awareness and building strong connections between the organization and the local community. With a background in outreach and communication, Sierra focuses on using social media, organizing events, and running educational campaigns to promote mental health awareness. She is dedicated to ensuring Hope Harmony’s services reach those in need and creating an inclusive, supportive environment where individuals feel empowered to seek help. Sierra’s efforts are crucial in helping the community connect with valuable mental health resources.

Chris Pike – Volunteer Coordinator

Chris Pike is the Volunteer Coordinator at Hope Harmony, where he plays a vital role in managing and supporting the organization’s dedicated volunteers. With a strong passion for community service, Chris is responsible for recruiting, training, and coordinating volunteers, ensuring they have the resources and support needed to make a meaningful impact. He works closely with the team to ensure volunteers are engaged and aligned with Hope Harmony’s mission of providing support and resources to individuals facing mental health challenges. Isaac Cuthbert – Lead Mental Health Professional

Isaac Cuthbert is the Lead Mental Health Professional at Hope Harmony, where he oversees the organization’s mental health services and ensures that individuals receive the highest level of care and support. Isaac’s expertise and compassionate approach are central to Hope Harmony’s mission of providing comprehensive mental health support.
